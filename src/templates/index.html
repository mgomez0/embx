What is embx?

embx is a consultancy committed to supercharging your team's development practices. We pledge to give your firm the tools to confidently develop, test, and deploy embedded systems at a pace that rivals our pure software counterparts.

Who am I?

I'm an embedded software engineer that's worked at companies like Apple, Hewlett Packard Enterprise, and Sonos. I've worked on everything from bare-metal microcontrollers for low-power home security applications, to RTOS-based power monitoring on an ARM SoC (Apple Silicon), to userspace embedded Linux applications (Sonos audio distribution). I love C and Rust, but my current day job requires I use the sprawling mess that is C++. I'm also a big fan of Python for hacking together quick automation scripts. I consider myself a web hobbyist, and I built this blog entirely with Rust and Tera. I love the challenges and complexity of embedded systems, but despise all the antiquated processes that make the software development lifecycle very slow in comparison to our web/mobile/SaaS counterparts. I want to bring embedded development into modern times with cutting-edge tooling, fearless testing, reproducible builds, configurable environments, containerized dependencies, and more.
